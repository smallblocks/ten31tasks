# Ten31 Tasks — Instructions

## Setup

1. Open Ten31 Tasks from your StartOS dashboard
2. Go to the **Team** tab and add each team member by name
3. Share their personal URL: `tasks.ten31.ai/list/<name>`
4. Each person bookmarks their URL and uses it daily

## Daily Workflow

**Evening (the night before):**
1. Go to your URL
2. Write your six most important tasks for tomorrow — #1 is the most important
3. Tap **Commit List** to lock it in

**The next day:**
1. Work item 1 until it's done — do not touch item 2 until item 1 is complete
2. Check off tasks as you finish them
3. Unfinished items can be carried forward to tomorrow

**End of day:**
1. Write a short reflection on what got done and what didn't

## Auto-Save

Every keystroke, every check mark, every action saves immediately to the server.
There is no save button. Data persists across all browsers and devices.

## Team Board

The Team tab shows everyone's daily status:
- Who has committed their list today
- Their full task list with completion status
- Current streak and 7-day completion rate
- Tap any member to drill into their full history

## Data & Backup

All data is stored in a SQLite database on your StartOS volume.
Use the standard StartOS backup to preserve your data.
